Keyboard Bindings

Certain VR controller actions are available when a headset is not connected to the computer. The following are the keyboard actions that mimic the VR controllers.

Right Trigger: ]

Left Trigger: [

Right Grip: ‘

Left Grip: ;

Right Controller Tilted Up: o

Right Controller Tilted Down: ,

Right Controller Tilted Right: l

Right Controller Tilted Left: k

Left Controller Tilted Up: u

Left Controller Tilted Down: n

Left Controller Tilted Right: j

Left Controller Tilted Left: h



Move camera with the mouse, right click to turn off mouse movement
